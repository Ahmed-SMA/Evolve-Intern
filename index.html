Core principle

Warm entry. Clear system. Professional execution.

The first two days remain calm and orientation-focused. From Day 3 onward, interns start guided production. Structure increases gradually as confidence grows.

01

Onboard Softly

Welcome letter, icebreaker, company understanding, examples, questions and system setup without overwhelming them.

02

Work Practically

Interns learn through real creative work: design, content, social media, branding, video, UI/UX, proposals and events.

03

Document Everything

CRM, Drive, daily memorandums, reports and weekly reviews create clarity for interns and evidence for management.

System promise

They are not here just to complete tasks. They are here to build themselves.

This website acts as the shared reference point for both sides: interns understand what they will learn, while management understands how to execute and monitor the process.
