What interns will learn

A clear breakdown of skills, business exposure and tools that will be introduced during the internship.

SM

Social Media

Content calendars, captions, hashtags, posting formats, campaigns and basic boosting concepts.

GD

Graphic Design

Posts, carousels, brochures, visiting cards, letterheads, presentation graphics and brand consistency.

BR

Branding

Brand guidelines, product packaging, e-commerce brand themes, profiles and prospectus design.

VX

Video & Experience

Video production, podcasts, reels, documentary basics, event coverage and editing workflows.

UI

UI/UX

Website layouts, wireframes, interface thinking, user flows and industry-based design concepts.

BD

Business Skills

Proposals, reports, client profiles, case studies, client meetings and presentation skills.

EV

Events

Talking Books, The Real Debate, Cultural Evolution Conference, planning, execution and coverage.

AI

AI & Tools

ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Canva AI, Figma AI, CRM workflows, Google Workspace and smarter execution.

AI is not a shortcut. It is a productivity system.

Interns will learn how to use AI responsibly for research, ideation, caption writing, visual direction, documentation, scripting and workflow efficiency.
