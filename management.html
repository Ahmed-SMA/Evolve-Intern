For Management

Internship operating system

This page explains how Evolve Digital will manage the full process without confusion, scattered communication or missing deliverables.

A

Assign

Tasks are assigned through CRM with deadlines, references, status, priority and reviewer.

B

Collect

Final exports, source files and reports are uploaded to Drive using the structured folder system.

C

Evaluate

Daily memorandums and weekly reviews become evidence for feedback, portfolio and completion reports.

Control flow

How work moves through the system

Management Actions

  • Create task in CRM
  • Attach references and deadline
  • Review submitted work
  • Request revisions if needed
  • Approve final assets
  • Record weekly progress

Intern Actions

  • Check CRM daily
  • Submit morning memorandum
  • Complete assigned tasks
  • Upload final and source files
  • Submit end-of-day report
  • Reflect weekly on learning
Documents

Documents required for control and clarity

Welcome letter, internship guide, roadmap, communication guide and team directory.
Daily memorandum, end-of-day report, attendance, CRM task board and asset submission register.
Proposal templates, company profile templates, case study templates, client meeting notes and event coverage formats.
Portfolio checklist, evaluation form, completion report, recommendation letter and certificate.
